How it began

Our Journey

◆

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is our favorite. Here's a little of how it unfolded.

2019

We First Crossed Paths

We first crossed paths through mutual friends, long before it was anything more. In late 2019, a group trip to Tagaytay — captured together in a group photo, though never just the two of us

2024

Together

On June 2024, everything changed. What had quietly been friendship became something more, and we officially began our journey as a couple.

2024–2025

Our Adventures

From Calatagan to Pansol, Zambales to Baguio, and countless days in Tagaytay and Antipolo — our favorite squad was always along for the ride, turning every trip into a new chapter that brought us closer together.

2025

The Proposal

On December 7, 2025, on the shores of Boracay — our first trip together by plane — Khen got down on one knee, and Justine said yes to forever.
